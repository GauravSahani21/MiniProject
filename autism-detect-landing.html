Know the Signs
Early Signs of Autism in Preschool Children
👁️
Limited Eye Contact
Avoiding eye contact during conversations or play interactions with others.
🤝
Social Withdrawal
Preference to play alone; difficulty engaging in group activities or peer play.
🔁
Repetitive Behaviors
Repetitive hand-flapping, rocking, spinning objects, or fixed routines.
💬
Speech Delays
Delayed language development or loss of previously acquired speech milestones.
👂
Not Responding to Name
Consistently not turning or responding when their name is called by a caregiver.
🧩
Intense Fixation
Very strong focus on specific topics, objects, or routines to the exclusion of other things.
😟
Sensory Sensitivity
Over- or under-reaction to sounds, light, textures, tastes, or temperature changes.
👋
Limited Gestures
Not pointing, waving, or using other gestures to communicate by 12 months.
